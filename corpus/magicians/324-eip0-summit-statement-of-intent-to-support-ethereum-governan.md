# EIP0 Summit:  Statement of intent to support Ethereum governance initiatives

https://medium.com/eip0-summit/eip0-summit-statement-of-intent-to-support-ethereum-governance-initiatives-e54ff782933

> The signers of this public statement commit to supporting one or more of the following next steps:
>
>
> Work on a statement of shared values of Ethereum
> Support and build open source tools to collect key signals and metrics
> Host a monthly governance call
> Organize a second #EIP0 Summit
