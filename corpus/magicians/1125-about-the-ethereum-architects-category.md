# About the Ethereum Architects category

**Ring of Ethereum Architects**

EA keeps in mind that most core projects in the Ethereum ecosystem with focus on their interoperability, performance and specific features. EA’s role in a project is to integrate different modules into working product and compare similar modules to pick the most suitable one.

In the Ethereum community, EA takes care of a core project’s UX, interoperability and integrations and creates RFEs in those projects in order to push necessary improvements. In Ethereum governance (Signaling), his role is to identify influencers (projects and developers).

The Ring of Ethereum Architects supports, summarizes and promotes community work done by its members.

For more information, see: https://github.com/ethereum-magicians/scrolls/wiki/Ring-of-Ethereum-Architects
