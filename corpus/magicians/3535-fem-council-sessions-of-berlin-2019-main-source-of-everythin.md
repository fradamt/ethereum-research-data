# FEM Council sessions of Berlin 2019 (Main Source of Everything)

Hello all!

This is a sum-up post; we will try to keep it as the Main Source of Everything related to FEM Council that will happen around [Berlin Blockchain Week](https://www.blockchainweek.berlin/).

We will have two 3hr+ gatherings in Berlin during Blockchain week, and a lot of Ring Meetings!

---

## FEM Main Council

Main Council will be more general and we are expecting that more people will attend this Council

**When:** August 19th

**Time:** 2pm till 5pm (with break)

**Where:** [Berlin Institute of Technology](https://www.google.com/maps/place/Berlin+Institute+of+Technology/@52.5125322,13.3247559,17z/data=!3m1!4b1!4m5!3m4!1s0x47a8511b39800a71:0xb40aec2d36688d81!8m2!3d52.5125322!4d13.3269446)

### To attend Main Council:

- Register via FEM Council Berlin 2019 Registration form
- Buy ticket MetaCartel Demo Day (0.1ETH)

**Main Council Agenda**

- State of the Magicians
- Business Models
- Education & Future of Work
- Decentralising Data Access –> move to Tuesday @ Web3 Summit
- ProgPOW –> move to a community call?

> Please add resources to this full list of proposed topics:
> Proposed Topics Overview - Berlin 2019 - Main Council - HackMD

---

## FEM “More Magicians”

“More Magicians” will be more focused on Ethereum governance-related topics and topics affecting the wider ecosystem (beyond the Ethereum protocol).

**When:** August 20th

**Time:** 11am - 3pm

**Where:** [Funkhaus](https://www.google.com/maps/place/Funkhaus+Berlin+Nalepastrasse/@52.4795432,13.4980899,17z/data=!3m1!4b1!4m5!3m4!1s0x47a84f2e154b6355:0x163f2116c5300be2!8m2!3d52.4795432!4d13.5002786) in the “Hackerspace” room

### To attend “More Magicians”:

- Register via FEM Council Berlin 2019 Registration form
- Buy ticket on Web3 Summit

**“More Magicians” Agenda**

- EIP-1559 - Fee market changes
- Interoperability Standards (across chain standards)
- Signaling
- Funding of infrastructure projects in a distributed ecosystem
- Decentralising Data Access
- Alternateth –> delay to a later time?
