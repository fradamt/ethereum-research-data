# ERC-7405: Portable Smart Contract Accounts

The proposed EIP introduces a standardized solution called “Portable Smart Contract Accounts” (PSCA) that aims to address the lack of portability and compatibility issues faced by Smart Contract Accounts (SCA) across different wallet providers.
