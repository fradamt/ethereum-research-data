# Mascot needed for Glamsterdam upgrade

A mascot is needed for Glamsterdam upgrade ([EIP-7773: Hardfork Meta - Glamsterdam](https://eips.ethereum.org/EIPS/eip-7773)).

[EIP-8066: Upgrade Mascots](https://ethereum-magicians.org/t/eip-8066-upgrade-mascots/26009) specifies mascot requirements and role of facilitator (Mascot Wrestler).

[@EvilJordan](/u/eviljordan) proposed as Glamsterdam Mascot Wrestler

Recent upgrade names:

- Shapella (Shanghai + Capella)
- Dencun (Cancun + Deneb)
- Pectra (Prague + Electra)
- Fusaka (Fulu + Osaka)
- Glamsterdam (Gloas + Amsterdam)

See: [Post-Merge Network Upgrade Naming Schemes](https://ethereum-magicians.org/t/rfc-post-merge-network-upgrade-naming-schemes/11977)
