# Join the 3rd AllERCDevs 2023-06-13 Tuesday UTC2300

If you care about [#SmartContracts](https://twitter.com/hashtag/SmartContracts?src=hashtag_click) [#Interoperability](https://twitter.com/hashtag/Interoperability?src=hashtag_click), [#OpenStandards](https://twitter.com/hashtag/OpenStandards?src=hashtag_click) please join us in the 3rd session of [#AllERCDevs](https://twitter.com/hashtag/AllERCDevs?src=hashtag_click) where [#ERC](https://twitter.com/hashtag/ERC?src=hashtag_click) Authors, [#dApps](https://twitter.com/hashtag/dApps?src=hashtag_click) builders join to show and learn from each other, solicit and give technical feedback, and promote adoption.
