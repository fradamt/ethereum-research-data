# PeerDAS breakout #11, October 29 2024

#### Agenda

[PeerDAS Breakout Room #11 · Issue #1183 · ethereum/pm · GitHub](https://github.com/ethereum/pm/issues/1183)

#### Summary

*[Copied from Eth R&D [Discord](https://discord.com/channels/595666850260713488/988798065894907934/1301034576537452575)]*

- Client teams have been working on sync refactoring, pectra rebase, various optimization and devnet issues.
- peerdas-devnet-3 is in a pretty bad state and will be shut down today. peerdas-devnet-4 has been scheduled for end of November.
- Client teams discussed whether to activate PeerDAS at the Fulu fork, and agreed to discuss with the wider group in the next ACD.
- Discussions on various spec changes: consensus-spec#3832, consensus-spec#3993, and activation of EIP-7742. Client teams to review the PRs and provide feedback.
- Discussed PeerDAS metric PRs, client teams to review and provide feedback.
- The next breakout call will be cancelled due to devcon, next call will be on the 26th Nov.
